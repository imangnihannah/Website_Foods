        <img src="Creamy%20Pinoy%20Fried%20Rice.png"/><img id="img2" src="banner.png"><img id="img3" src="serve.jpg"> <p id="p3">Preparation:</p>
        <br>
        <p id="p4">1. Heat butter in a heavy saucepan. Add the longganisa and cook until<br>
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;slightly brown. Add onions and garlic. Sauté until it begins to soften. Do<br>
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;not brown. Add pineapple. Then add stock or water. Season with salt and <br>
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;pepper. Simmer for 2 minutes.<br><br>
        2. Add NESTLÉ All Purpose Cream. Stir and simmer again for 3 minutes.<br><br>
        3. Add the rice. Stir over low heat until the rice is completely coated. Simmer<br>
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;for a minute. Taste and adjust seasoning; cover pan tightly with foil.<br><br>
        4. Add the bell peppers and green peas just before the liquid is absorbed.<br>
        &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then cover again until the rice is thoroughly cooked.<br><br>
        5.Top with sliced salted egg and garnish with spring onions.</p>
        <p id="p5">Servings 6-8<br>
        Preparation Time 45 minutes</p>
        <p id="p6">CREAMY PINOY FRIED RICE</p>
    </div>
    <div id="second">
        <p id="p1">Ingredients:</p>

        <p id="p2">¾ cup      &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;pineapple tidbits salt and pepper to taste</p>
        <p id="p2">1 pack     &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;NESTLÉ All Purpose Cream 250ml</p>
        <p id="p2">3 cups     &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;cooked rice</p>

        <p id="p2">2 pcs      &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;salted egg</p>
