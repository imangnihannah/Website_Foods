Chicken Sisig

Servings 7
Preparation Time 30 minutes
Cooking Time 1 hour and 10 minutes

Ingredients:

• 400 g chicken, breast fillet, cut into 1/4-inch cubes
• 150 g chicken liver, cut into 1/4-inch cubes
• reserved pineapple syrup
• 1/2 tsp salt
• 2 Tbsp oil, for sautéing
• 1/2 cup onion, white, sliced
• 2 Tbsp garlic, crushed
• 1/2 cup bell pepper, green, chopped
• 1/2 cup bell pepper, red, chopped
• 1 pc siling haba, chopped
• 1 pouch DEL MONTE Pineapple Tidbits (200g), drained,
reserve syrup
• 4 tsp liquid seasoning
• 2 1/2 tsp sinigang mix
• salt, to taste
• pepper, to taste
• 1 pc egg

Preparation

1. Marinate chicken breast and liver in pineapple syrup and salt for 30
minutes in the refrigerator. Drain then set aside.

2. Sauté onion, garlic, chicken and liver over high heat for 8 minutes.
Add bell peppers and sili. Sauté for 2 minutes.

3. Add DEL MONTE Pineapple Tidbits, liquid seasoning, sinigang mix
and salt and pepper to taste. Cook over high heat, continuously
stirring for about 3 minutes or until slightly dry.

4. Add egg at the center, then stir. Remove from flame. Serve with
calamansi if desired.

Cooking Skills Needed

Cooking Tools Needed
