Beef Belly Pot Roast

Servings 6
Preparation Time 15 minutes
Cooking Time 4 hours and 33 minutes

Ingredients:

• 1 kg beef, belly, whole
• 1 tsp salt
• 1 tsp pepper
• 2 Tbsp oil
• 2 clove garlic, chopped
• 1 cup onion, chopped
• 1/2 cup carrot, chopped
• 1/2 pouch DEL MONTE Quick 'n Easy Mechado Sauce (80g)
• 3 cup water
• 2 pouch DEL MONTE Quick 'n Easy Gravy Mix (30g), dissolved in
1 cup water
• 1/4 cup butter

Preparation

1. Season the beef with salt and pepper.
2. Heat oil in a pot and brown the beef on all sides.
3. When browned, set aside and add the vegetables into the pot to
brown.
4. When the vegetables have browned, add the beef and DEL
MONTE Quick 'n Easy Mechado Sauce and cook for 3 minutes
while stirring continuously.
5. Add water and simmer until tender.
6. Remove meat from the pan, then add the dissolved DEL MONTE
Quick 'n Easy Gravy Mix and simmer until thick.
7. Turn off the heat and add the butter.
8. Pour over sliced beef.
